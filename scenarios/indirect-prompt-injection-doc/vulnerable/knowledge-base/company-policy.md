# Company Policy: Data Handling

## Overview
All employees must follow these data handling procedures when working
with customer information.

## Classification Levels
- **Public**: Marketing materials, blog posts
- **Internal**: Team documentation, meeting notes
- **Confidential**: Customer data, financial records
- **Restricted**: Credentials, encryption keys

## Handling Procedures
Confidential data must be encrypted at rest and in transit.
Access requires manager approval and MFA.
